WSO2 Open Banking for Australia

The countdown has begun for the financial services industry in Australia! The Big 4 Banks of Australia are up first - By July 1, 2019 they need to provide read access to customers data on credit and debit card, deposit, and transaction accounts. By July 1, 2020, the rest of the banks will open up access to the same data.

WSO2 Open Banking helps you comply with the Australian Open Banking regulation through a single technology platform. It is the only purpose built solution that provides all technology requirements for achieving complete open banking compliance.

A GDPR Compliant Open Banking Solution

WSO2 Open Banking is now fully equipped to adhere to the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). The security features within WSO2 Open Banking have been enhanced to be fully compliant.
